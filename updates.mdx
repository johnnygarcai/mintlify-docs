## July 8, 2026

**A clearer sidebar.** The steward dashboard's navigation is now a short, direct list (**Dashboard**, **Projects**, **People**, **Approvals**, **Donations**), with organization **Settings** and **Plans & billing** moved to a **Settings** button on the Dashboard page. The **Reporting** page is now called **Approvals**.

**In-app notifications.** A small badge next to a sidebar item lets you know when something needs attention or there's an unread update: a new team member joining, a verification result, a report ready for review. Notifications also appear as dismissible cards near the top of the relevant page.

**A running donations ledger.** The new **Donations** page tracks money received from donors, grants, and matching gifts, designated to a project or held in your general fund. This is a manual record for reporting purposes; Mirage doesn't move money.

**Bulk email invitations.** Invite your whole team at once by pasting a list of email addresses on the **People** page, instead of adding them one at a time. Pending invites now show a clear delivery status.

**Project lifecycle tools.** Clone a project's metrics, tasks, and questions into a new draft with **Clone project**; reports and funding never come along. Projects with submitted field reports can no longer be deleted outright; archive them instead to keep the record.

**Map view for projects.** Switch your **Projects** page to **Map view** to see every project plotted by location and status.

**Clearer verification.** Give your verification documents a name instead of seeing a raw file ID, and open any submitted document to double-check it. Status badges are now color-coded so you can see at a glance whether you're verified, pending, or need to resubmit.

**New project wizard polish.** Creating a project now walks through a short, clearer set of steps, with a website field added alongside name, description, hero image, and location.

**Field app improvements.** Based on tester feedback: task buttons on your dashboard now clearly show whether a task is unstarted, in progress, or submitted; the observation notes field grows as you type; and completing a report signs and encrypts it automatically, no signature to draw.
