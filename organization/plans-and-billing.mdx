Billing lives on your organization's **Plans** page and is managed by stewards.

## Plans

| Plan | How to get it |
| --- | --- |
| **Standalone** | Self-serve — subscribe directly from the Plans page with a card. |
| **Enterprise / Custom** | Sales-led — arranged with the Mirage team for larger organizations, networks, or custom requirements. |

Current pricing is listed on the [pricing page](https://app.mirage.partners/pricing).

## Subscribe to Standalone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start checkout">
    On the **Plans** page, choose Standalone. If you have a promotion or referral code, apply it here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay securely">
    You're taken to a Stripe-hosted checkout to enter payment details. Mirage never sees or stores your card number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    You're returned to the dashboard with your subscription active. The Plans page always shows your current status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage your subscription

From the Plans page, **Manage billing** opens your Stripe customer portal, where you can:

- Update your payment method
- Download invoices
- Cancel your subscription

Subscription status (active, trialing, past due, canceled) syncs to Mirage automatically. If a payment fails, Stripe retries and your stewards are notified so you can update the card before access is affected.

## Referrals

The **Refer** page lets you send a referral to another nonprofit — they get a discount code for their own subscription. Enter their name, email, and organization, add a personal note if you like, and Mirage sends it along.

<Note>
  Questions about enterprise plans, seats, or invoicing? Email [contact@mirage.partners](mailto:contact@mirage.partners) — enterprise and custom plans are set up manually, without self-serve checkout.
</Note>
