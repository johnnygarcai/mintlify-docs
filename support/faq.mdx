## Accounts & organizations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can one person belong to multiple organizations?">
    Yes. Use the organization switcher in the dashboard sidebar. Roles are per-organization: you can be a steward in one and a reporter in another.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a steward also submit field reports?">
    Yes. Stewards can submit reports against their own organization's tasks, which is handy for testing a new task before deploying it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I remove someone from my organization?">
    Manage members from the **People** page. Deactivating an invite code doesn't remove reporters who already joined with it; remove them individually.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Reporters & tasks

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My reporter joined but doesn't see any tasks. Why?">
    Check three things: the reporter successfully joined **your** organization (they appear on your People page), the project is **Active** (draft and paused projects don't surface tasks), and the task hasn't hit its response **capacity**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="An invite code isn't working. What do I check?">
    Codes stop working when they're **deactivated**, **expired**, or have reached their **maximum uses**, all visible on the People page. Generate a fresh code if in doubt; codes are free.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do reporters need to download anything from an app store?">
    No. The reporter app runs in the browser at [report.mirage.partners](https://report.mirage.partners) and installs to the home screen as a web app on both iPhone and Android.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if reporters work in another language?">
    Reporters set their languages in their profile, and audio evidence is transcribed and translated to English automatically: testimony in Kiswahili or Spanish arrives on your review screen in both the original and English.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Metrics & review

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A report was submitted but my metrics didn't change. Why?">
    Metrics only move when contributions are **approved**. Check the **Approvals** queue: extracted contributions are probably sitting in pending. (Extraction itself takes a few minutes after submission.)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The AI extracted a wrong value. What do I do?">
    Reject that contribution: it's excluded from your aggregates. The AI's confidence score and source link exist so you can catch exactly this during review.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit metric values by hand?">
    No, that's by design. Every number in Mirage traces to reviewed field evidence. If a value is missing, ask a reporter to submit it; if it's wrong, reject it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Donor reports

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can see a donor report?">
    Anyone with the link: donor reports are public pages with no login. Only steward-approved data and consented media appear on them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I keep a specific photo off public pages?">
    Media marked **internal only** (or without **face permission**) at capture time is excluded from donor-facing pages automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do donor reports update as new data comes in?">
    Your choice at generation: **live update** reports re-aggregate on every view; **frozen snapshots** stay fixed. See [Generating donor reports](/donor-reports/generating-donor-reports#live-vs-frozen).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel or change my subscription?">
    **Plans → Manage billing** opens your Stripe portal, where you can update payment details, download invoices, or cancel. See [Plans & billing](/organization/plans-and-billing).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="We're a large organization or network. Is there an enterprise option?">
    Yes. Enterprise and custom plans are arranged directly with the Mirage team rather than through self-serve checkout.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
