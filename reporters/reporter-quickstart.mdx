<Info>
  This section is written for **field reporters** — the people capturing evidence on the ground. Stewards: share these two pages with your reporters when you [deploy them](/organization/team-and-field-reporters).
</Info>

## What you'll need

- A smartphone with a camera (any modern iPhone or Android)
- The **invite code** or link your organization gave you — a 6-character code like `PQXN8K`

## Get set up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the reporter app">
    Go to [report.mirage.partners](https://report.mirage.partners) in your phone's browser and sign up with your email or Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join your organization">
    On the **Join** screen, enter your invite code (or just open the link your organization sent — the code fills in automatically). You're in: the organization's tasks now appear on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the app on your home screen">
    Mirage Reporter is a web app that installs like a native one:

    - **iPhone**: in Safari, tap the share button, then **Add to Home Screen**.
    - **Android**: tap **Install** when prompted, or use the browser menu → **Install app**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your languages (optional)">
    In your **Profile**, set your primary language and any additional languages you speak. You can also enable **anonymous mode** if you prefer your name not to be shown to the organization's staff.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your dashboard

- **Active projects** — the initiatives you can report on, with photos and locations.
- **Map** — every task site, with directions one tap away.
- **Tasks due** — what needs doing, most urgent first. Each task shows its reward value and what evidence is needed.
- **Reports** — everything you've submitted and its status.
- **Impact** — your running totals: reports submitted, projects contributed to, regions covered.

Ready to report? Continue to [Submitting field reports](/reporters/submitting-field-reports).
