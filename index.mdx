Mirage connects on-the-ground reality with donor conviction. Your field reporters capture photos, video, audio, and answers from where the work happens; Mirage's AI analyzes that evidence and extracts verified metrics; you review and approve everything; and one click turns it all into a beautiful, shareable impact report your donors can actually trust.

These docs are written primarily for **nonprofit teams using the Steward Dashboard** — the people setting up projects, deploying field reporters, reviewing submissions, and sharing results with donors.

## Why teams use Mirage

- **Evidence, not anecdotes.** Every number in a donor report traces back to real field submissions — photos, recordings, and answers reviewed by your team.
- **AI does the heavy lifting.** Mirage transcribes audio, summarizes media, and proposes metric values with confidence scores. You stay in control: nothing counts until a steward approves it.
- **Minutes, not months.** Once reports are flowing, generating a polished, donor-ready impact page is one click.
